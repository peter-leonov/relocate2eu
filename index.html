See how we can help you

Relocate2.eu is a Team of relocation specialists with IT- and management background located in Germany. We have set ourselves the goal to erase global labor market borders and make it highly accessable for the qualified IT and IT related professionals. 

Brush aside all doubts and open a new page of your life with our help. Our solid experience and well-organized working processes will make your relocation as comfortable and easy as possible. You can rely on our support and professional service irrespective of which part of Germany you are going to live in. 

NEW OPPORTUNITIES

Relocation to Germany means both, a new page of your and your family’s life and a new quality of life.  You will be pleasantly impressed by a vast range of advantages. In Germany you can expect: social benefits, safe and stable future for your children, unique experience of working in the world’s famous companies and concerns, career growth, decent salary and affordable medicine.  You will be able to open up new horizons with Blue Card visa which allows you to live and work in any country of Europe.

PROFESSIONAL SUPPORT

As a professional service provider, we undertake relocation of IT experts from everywhere around the world to the EU countries and their integration into the European labor market. We make sure the communication during the application process works smoothly and provide safe and reliable individual support from the very beginning, the moment right after the job offer was accepted, until the actual job start in the new company.  We also ensure a quick and easy procedure of regulatory matters before and after the relocation, provide additional on-site apartment search support and help expats with the bank insurance and other affairs.

CONTEMPORARY APPROACH

We offer integrated solutions in order to make relocation process less complicated.  We provide individual support in searching the job and housing in Germany, learning German with professionals and native speakers, opening the Visa...  We use modern software solutions to make the processes more efficient. Our goal is to organize complicated sequences and to bring more transparency into the relocation process.
